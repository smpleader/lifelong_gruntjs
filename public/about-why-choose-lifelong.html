Why choose lifelong?

Home // About // Why Choose LifeLong?
Most of all because we care about adoption - and further, LGBT adoption. As adoptive parents, we know first hand the pain of infertility and the miracle of adopting a baby. Let us help you bring your child home. As an LGBT friendly adoption service, we look forward to helping you find your miracle.

Why choose LifeLong Adoptions?

Our experience

We have a staff with over 20 years of experience.

Personal service

Safe, personalized service, from a team to guide you through your adoption process and adopting a baby.

PLACEMENT Times

LifeLong Adoptions works with a limited number of couples, resulting in shorter placement times.

24/7 availability

Our staff works with birthmothers 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

BBB rating

LifeLong Adoptions has an "A+" rating with the Better Business Bureau for its outstanding service and professionalism.

No wait lists

We do not believe in "wait lists", and therefore do not present client profiles to birthmothers based on a "wait list" position.

Continuous Marketing

Should a connection fail, we will continue our marketing and outreach efforts on your behalf at no additional cost to you, until you have a successful adoption, within your two year contract.

Nationwide Outreach

LifeLong Adoptions provides an aggressive nationwide advertising, marketing, and national outreach campaign for potential birthmothers, resulting in shorter placement times.

Unique Profiles

We create your unique adoption profile to get you chosen by a birthmother; printing and distribution included.

Adoption resources

We provide the latest and most informative resources available to keep you educated on LGBT adoption and the adoption process.

LGBT Friendly

We are an LGBT, Gay & Lesbian and same-sex friendly service.

Here to Help You

We are here to help you find the baby that was meant for you.

As a gay and lesbian adoption friendly agency, LifeLong Adoptions believes in LGBT adoption, and specializes in assisting LGBT couples and individuals. Our philosophy is based on one-on-one guidance, providing adoptive parents with an adoption specialist to guide you through the process.
