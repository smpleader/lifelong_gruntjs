Our Mission
LifeLong Adoptions is committed to creating families through adoption by preparing, nurturing, supporting, and advocating for all those involved in the infant adoption process.

We proudly work

by the following

core values:

FAMILY

We believe in the power and significance of family and that one can be formed in many different ways. As we are creating families through adoption, we are also building our own LifeLong family. When you work with us, you become part of our extended adoption family.

CARE

Incredibly passionate about adoption, we work with our hearts first. We genuinely care about our birthmothers and adoptive parents and work tirelessly to ensure they are well taken care of throughout the entire adoption process.

COMPASSION

We understand how rough the road to parenthood can be, especially for those who battled infertility or who have not always had equal rights. We also have deep respect for our birthmothers and provide them with compassionate, non-judgmental support.

SUPPORT

Our team is dedicated to guiding you through every step of your adoption journey. We do everything in our power to make the process as easy as possible for all involved.

EQUALITY

As a gay-friendly adoption service, we fully support the LGBT community in their pursuit of adoption. We believe everyone deserves to achieve their dream of starting or continuing a family through adoption.

INTEGRITY

We pride ourselves in conducting our business activities with an unwavering commitment to honesty and integrity.

ADVOCACY

We envision a whole community in which everyone views adoption as a wonderful, natural and joyous way of creating a family. We work hard to spread the positive message about adoption and raise adoption awareness in our community and beyond.

As a gay and lesbian adoption friendly agency, LifeLong Adoptions believes in LGBT adoption, and specializes in assisting LGBT couples and individuals. Our philosophy is based on one-on-one guidance, providing adoptive parents with an adoption specialist to guide you through the process.
