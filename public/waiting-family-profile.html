We can only imagine all the emotions you may be experiencing, and are honored by your courage to consider adoption as an option for this child. Together, we have always dreamed of adopting a child, and we couldn’t be more humbled than to have you as a part of our family. If you allow us, we would be honored to offer unconditional love to this child, to be nurturing and protective along with lifelong learning, worldly exposure and encouragement to be his or her best self.

As parents, we will teach the importance of humility, thankfulness, appreciation, tolerance and self-respect. Living in a beautiful and diverse neighborhood in the city, we will show this child the value of diversity and being included. Your child will always be loved and safe in our home with our two friendly dogs. Our ultimate goal is to create an environment for your child to grow into a responsible, loving, and nurturing person.

Thank you for taking the time to read our story and learn more about us. We look forward to learning more about the life you envision us creating together for your child. We would be honored to have you consider us part of your journey.

About Us

Matt is a technology consultant and is truly passionate about his job. He has worked in over 20 countries and despite his travel, he regularly works from home and has lots of vacation time to spend with our family. He is also a fabulous chef, and makes exquisite meals for us, and our family and friends every weekend.

Andrew is an American history teacher and teaches 10th and 11th grade students. He has a love of learning and of teaching. He is well read, an avid runner, and enjoys living a healthy and active lifestyle.

Together, we enjoy swimming in the pool in the summer, running in the winter, and we always enjoy traveling to new and exciting places. We regularly work together on home improvement projects, and we always enjoy spending time with our two rescue dogs – Peanut and Tiberius, who can’t wait for a little one to play and cuddle with!

Our Home

Our neighborhood is truly special because while we live inside the fourth largest city in the country, yet it is a quiet and desirable part of the city. We live within walking distance to the largest park in Houston and we are constantly walking there from our house with our two gentle dogs. We take great pride that our neighborhood is extremely diverse, and also has highly ranked schools. We are also within 15 minutes of downtown Houston, which is full of museums, restaurants and other fun attractions for a child.

Our Extended Family

Our families do not live in Texas with us, yet our entire family still spends a significant amount of time together, happily celebrating life and special occasions with each other. Our parents are ecstatic about becoming grandparents!

Andrew’s parents live in South Florida, moments from the ocean, where they are excited to teach our child to swim. Matt’s parents live in the Canadian Rockies where they are excited to hike and share a love a nature.

Our parents regularly visit us in Houston, and are already making plans to visit several times per year to visit their grandchild. We look forward to the trips we will take with our child to see their Grandparents on beaches of Florida, and in the mountains in Canada.

What Led Us to Adoption

Since we met, we have always dreamed of adopting a child. In our time together we have lovingly renovated our home, continually making improvements as we prepared for a child. We share a love of travel, culture and cuisine, and have travelled extensively together. We are excited to share our love of travel and the knowledge of other places and cultures with a child. We work hard for each other and the future of our family, which we hope involves children to share everything we have accomplished.
