LGBT adoption Laws Alabama

Home // LGBT Adoption // LGBT Adoption Laws Alabama

The state of Alabama permits single LGBT individuals to petition to adopt?

Yes.

The state of Alabama permits a same-sex couple to jointly petition to adopt?

Yes.

The state of Alabama permits a same-sex partner to petition to adopt partner’s child or child of the relationship?

No explicit prohibition.
