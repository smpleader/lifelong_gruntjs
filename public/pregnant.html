considering adoption?

So you're pregnant, and it wasn't planned. There is help for you here.

Our counseling specialists will provide the support and information you need to determine which option is best for you. Should you choose to place your baby for adoption through LifeLong Adoptions, our counselors will be with you every step of the way.

If you’re thinking about adoption, there are a few things we want you to know first:

Adoption is your decision. Only you can choose what's best for you and your baby.
Financial assistance with housing and other pregnancy-related expenses is available.
We're here to help you make your decision. No judgement, no pressure, and no rush.
All of our services are 100% free, confidential, safe, and legal.

Talk to an adoption counselor now at 1-888-733-7754, or fill out the form below to receive free adoption information.
